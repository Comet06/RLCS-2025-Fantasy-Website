Overall Rules

Rule 1: Prizes will be distributed based on the teams total points at the end of each split and after the championship(3 total, $200 per major, $500 for Championship, Plus additions from contributing players).

Rule 2: Prize pool TBD at the beginning of each season and subject to change.



Drafting Rules

Rule 1: Draft will occur at the beginning of the season, a total of 3 will be drafted, 1 for each position (Redraft may occur due to additional joining the league after Each major).

Rule 2: All drafted will need to be from different rank levels S --> F.

Rule 3: Player trades, swaps and all other changes must be made before Both of the regions in question have already played, or have not played yet.

Rule 4: Teams and positions will need to be submitted before the teams regionals have started in order for the changes to take effect, if no submission is made, previous submissions will be in effect.



Matchup Rules

Rule 1: Each Open Qualifier you will go up against another member in the group(each member will play 4 other members once determined randomly).

Rule 2: Each major and worlds #1 seed will play #2 seed, #3 vs #4, etc. (seeds are based by points).

Rule 3: Teams with the most points after each major wins that split.

Rule 4: Ties are settled by point values of the players on each team.
